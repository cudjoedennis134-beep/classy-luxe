Our Story

Born from a
Passion for Elegance

Classy Luxe was born from one simple, powerful belief: every person deserves to smell extraordinary. Founded in Accra, Ghana, our brand was created to bring world-class luxury fragrances within reach of every Ghanaian.

Our founder — a passionate fragrance enthusiast from Alhaji Lapaz — saw a gap: premium scents that were authentic, accessible, and delivered with genuine care. From that vision, Classy Luxe was born.

Today we carry Luxury Perfumes, Body Splash, Oil Perfumes, Deodorants, Diffusers, Hand Cream, Mini Wipes, and Petal Paper Soap — a complete range for those who believe in smelling extraordinary every day.
